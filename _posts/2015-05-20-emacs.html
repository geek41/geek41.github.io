使用emacs的一点心得,笔记.

1 Query replacing

Type Space or `y' to replace one match, Delete or `n' to skip to next, RET or `q' to exit, Period to replace one match and exit, Comma to replace but not move point immediately, C-r to enter recursive edit (C-M-c to get out again), C-w to delete match and recursive edit, C-l to clear the screen, redisplay, and offer same replacement again, ! to replace all remaining matches in this buffer with no more questions, ^ to move point back to previous match, E to edit the replacement string. In multi-buffer replacements type `Y' to replace all remaining matches in all remaining buffers with no more questions, `N' to skip to the next buffer without replacing remaining matches in the current buffer.
